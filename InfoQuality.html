Information quality

Information quality refers to the measure of quality of information. Information quality is comprised of multiple attributes such that if the writer of the information conforms to the attributes then the information will be of good quality to a certail category of information.

Characteristics that determine information quality

  1. Accuracy
  2. Relevence
  3. Completeness
  4. Consistency
  5. Credibility
  6. Clearity
  7. Objectivity

Accuracy

Accuracy refers to the degree to which information reflects the true and correct state of affairs, It measures how closely the information aligns with reality and whether it is free from errors and mistakes. High accuracy implies that information is reliable and can be trusted.
